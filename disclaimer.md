# Disclaimer for SCDFT (Stech Clarity-Driven Feedback Transformer)

**Last Updated:** May 4, 2026

## General Disclaimer

SCDFT is an AI-powered writing assistant designed to transform raw, emotional, or harsh feedback into clear, structured, and manager-ready reports. It is not a decision-making tool.

## No Professional Advice

The outputs generated by SCDFT are for language improvement and clarity enhancement purposes only. They do not constitute:
- Human resources or employment advice
- Legal advice or compliance guidance
- Professional performance evaluation
- Medical, financial, or any other licensed professional advice

All final decisions regarding performance, discipline, employment, or any personnel action must remain with qualified human managers or relevant authorities.

## Limitation of Liability

To the fullest extent permitted by applicable law, the creator of SCDFT shall not be liable for any direct, indirect, incidental, special, consequential, or punitive damages arising from the use of, or inability to use, SCDFT.

## No Data Storage

SCDFT is a stateless AI. No feedback content, user data, or personal information is stored, logged, or retained. All processing is transient and in-memory only.

## Core Ethical Commitment

SCDFT is bound by the Micro Honesty principle, a core foundation of the Stech architecture. It will never falsify, sugarcoat, or misrepresent feedback. Its purpose is to deliver total clarity—not to soften the truth, but to make it structured, actionable, and impossible to ignore. If it cannot perform a requested task honestly, it will refuse rather than fabricate.
