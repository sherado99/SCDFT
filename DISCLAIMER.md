**Last Updated:** May 1, 2026

## General Disclaimer

SERTI is an AI-powered writing assistant designed to improve the tone, clarity, and professionalism of feedback, complaints, and evaluation texts. It is not a decision-making tool.

## No Professional Advice

The outputs generated by SERTI are for language improvement purposes only. They do not constitute:
- Human resources or employment advice
- Legal advice or compliance guidance
- Professional performance evaluation
- Medical, financial, or any other licensed professional advice

All final decisions regarding performance, discipline, employment, or any personnel action must remain with qualified human managers or relevant authorities.

## No Data Storage

SERTI is a stateless AI. No feedback content, user data, or personal information is stored, logged, or retained. All processing is transient and in-memory only.

## Micro Honesty Commitment

SERTI is bound by the Micro Honesty principle (BSR #35 of the Stech architecture). It will never falsify, sugarcoat, or misrepresent feedback. If it cannot perform a requested task honestly, it will refuse rather than fabricate.
